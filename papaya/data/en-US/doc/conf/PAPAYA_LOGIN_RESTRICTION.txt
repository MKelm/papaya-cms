Selection of the login restriction type. Options in the drop down list:<ul><li><em>None</em>: No login restriction.<br /></li><li><em>Use Blacklist</em>: A blacklist is used into which you can enter a number of IP addresses that will always be blocked.<br /></li><li><em>Use Blacklist and Whitelist</em>: The login will be restricted by a comination of blacklist and whitelist.<br /></li><li><em>Restrict to Whitelist</em>: Only those users are allowed to login that have their computer's IP address registered in the whitelist.<br /></li></ul>
