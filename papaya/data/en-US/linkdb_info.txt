<b>Moving a category / Link</b>
<ol >
<li>Select the category (Section 'Categories') / the link (Section 'Links') to move.</li>
<li>Click on <i>Cut category</i> or <i>Cut link</i>, respectively.</li>
<li>Select the target category where you want to place the cut-out category/link.</li>
<li>Click on the icon <i>Paste</i> associated with the category/link on the Clipboard.</li>
</ol>
