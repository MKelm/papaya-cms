<p>
  You can use the sitemap to navigate through the whole Page structure.
</p>
<p>
 <img src="pics/icons/16x16/actions/edit.png" border="0" width="16" height="16" alt="" align="absmiddle"/>
 You can jump directly to the page editing area by clicking on the Pen symbol.
 Click on the pen in order to jump directly to the editing view of the page.
</p>
<p>
 <img src="pics/icons/16x16/items/publication.png" border="0" width="16" height="16" alt="" align="absmiddle"/> 
  You can publish the page immediately by clicking on the globe.
</p>
<p>
  Also, pages can be moved or copied to the Sitemap.<br/>
  To copy/move a page, hold the left mouse button down while dragging the page to the target position. 
  If you want to move a page, hold down the shift key whilst dragging.
  The mouse pointer will show you the current drag &amp; drop status by changing the pointer symbol. 
  When you release the mouse button, you will be asked to confirm the page moving/copying.
</p>
